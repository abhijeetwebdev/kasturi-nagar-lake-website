Our Story

From Neglect to Revival

Kasturi Nagar Lake, once a thriving ecosystem and the heart of our community, had fallen into neglect over the years. Pollution, encroachment, and lack of maintenance had transformed this beautiful water body into a shadow of its former self.

In 2023, a group of passionate residents decided enough was enough. What started as weekend cleanup drives soon evolved into a comprehensive rejuvenation project, bringing together volunteers from all walks of life.

Today, our lake is a testament to what a community can achieve when united by a common purpose. Every tree planted, every piece of trash removed, and every awareness campaign conducted represents the dedication of our volunteers who believe in preserving nature for future generations.

Our Work

Achievements So Far

Lake Cleanup

Removed over 5 tons of waste and debris from the lake and surrounding areas through regular cleanup drives.

Tree Plantation

Planted 1,000+ native trees and shrubs around the lake perimeter to prevent erosion and enhance biodiversity.

Water Treatment

Installed natural filtration systems and conducted regular water quality testing to ensure clean water.

Awareness Programs

Organized 30+ workshops and events to educate the community about environmental conservation.

Infrastructure

Built walking paths, benches, and viewing platforms to make the lake accessible to all community members.

Wildlife Return

Successfully attracted 25+ bird species back to the lake, indicating improved ecosystem health.

About Us

We are a community-driven organization dedicated to the restoration and preservation of Kasturi Nagar Lake. Founded by local residents who witnessed the deterioration of this precious water body, our group has grown into a movement of passionate environmentalists, volunteers, and concerned citizens.

Our team includes environmental scientists, educators, students, homemakers, and professionals from various fields, all united by a common goal: to restore our lake to its former glory and ensure its sustainability for future generations.

We believe in the power of collective action and community participation. Every contribution, whether it's time, expertise, or resources, makes a significant difference in our mission.
